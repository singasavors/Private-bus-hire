Frequently Asked Questions

What does the price include?

The price includes 5 days a week of transport service, covering both pickup from home to school and return from school to home.

Do you offer one-way transportation?

Yes, we do! If you only need transportation to school (morning) or from school (afternoon), you can opt for a one-way service. The cost will be half of the full price.

Are you a licensed company?

Yes, we are a licensed transportation provider. Our UEN is 53444733C, and we operate under KL Transportation Services, with positive customer reviews on Carousell.

Will my child be the only one on the bus?

No, there will be other children on the bus as we provide shared transportation services. However, we ensure a safe, comfortable, and well-supervised environment for all passengers.

Do you offer private bus hire for a single child?

Yes, we can arrange private transport for your child only, but the pricing will be different from the shared service. Please contact us for a customized quote.

How are the pickup and drop-off times determined?

Pickup and drop-off times are based on your child's school schedule and our route planning to ensure efficient and timely service. We will provide an estimated time upon booking.

What happens if my child is sick or absent?

If your child will not be taking the bus on a particular day, please inform us in advance. However, there will be no refunds or price adjustments for missed rides.

Can I request a change in pickup or drop-off location?

Yes, minor changes in pickup or drop-off locations can be accommodated within the same area. However, additional charges may apply for significant route changes.

What safety measures do you have in place?

We ensure all children are seated securely throughout the journey. Our buses are regularly maintained, and our drivers are experienced and background-checked to ensure the safety of all passengers.

How do I make a booking?

You can book our services by contacting us directly at +65 8058 1196 via phone or message. We will provide you with the necessary details and confirm your child’s slot.
