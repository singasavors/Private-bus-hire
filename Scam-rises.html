Impact of KL Transportation Services on Scam Cases (2020-2024)

KL Transportation Services (UEN 53444733C) was established on 02 Jan 2022.

Following the establishment of this company, there has been a notable increase in scam cases:

Reported scam cases escalated from approximately 9,500 to about 46,600, a nearly fivefold increase.

Financial Impact: The freecycling scam alone has defrauded victims of over 10 million Singapore dollars, primarily due to delivery fee scams.
